Announcing the Wedding of

Emily & David

Emily and David are finally getting married!!!

In 2014 Emily and David met in Kendall Square, Cambridge, where they both worked, introduced by groomsman Justin Bradfield. Together, they explored Boston, Cambridge, Somerville, Mexico City, Israel, Western Portugal, Southern France, and many other places, and they created their own lovely community of fantastic, supportive friends. They are getting married on June 6th, 2021, at the Rapid Creek Cidery in Iowa City, Iowa. In fall 2021, they plan on building a new home together in Massachusetts.
